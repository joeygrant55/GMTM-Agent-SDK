**Core Concept:** 24/7 AI recruiting coordinator that performs real actions for athletes (ages 8-25+)

## üéØ DISCOVERY & RESEARCH AGENTS

### 1. Camp & Combine Finder
**What it does:**
- Searches for upcoming camps/combines by sport, position, location, age group
- Filters by verified/legitimate events (avoid scams)
- Checks cost, date, coaches attending, past attendee outcomes
- Returns ranked list based on athlete's level + goals

**Data Sources:**
- GMTM event database
- MaxPreps event calendar
- College athletic websites
- SPARQ combine schedule
- Third-party showcase databases

**Output:**
- "Found 8 camps in your area this spring. Top 3: Houston SPARQ Combine (Feb 24, $75, 12 D2 scouts), Dallas Elite Showcase (Mar 5, $150, film reviews included)..."

---

### 2. College/Team Opportunity Matcher
**What it does:**
- Identifies programs recruiting athlete's position + level
- Checks roster needs (who's graduating, transfer portal moves)
- Finds club teams, semi-pro teams, Olympic development programs
- Tracks recruiting timelines and scholarship availability

**Categories:**
- **College:** D1, D2, D3, NAIA, JUCO
- **Club:** AAU, travel teams, elite clubs
- **Pro/Semi-Pro:** Arena leagues, overseas, developmental leagues
- **Olympic Pipeline:** National team tryouts, Olympic Training Centers, USOC programs

**Data Sources:**
- GMTM athlete/coach data
- College rosters + recruiting classes
- Transfer portal data
- USOC opportunity listings
- Club team directories

**Output:**
- "5 D2 programs in Texas are recruiting WRs. Houston Baptist needs speed (you're a fit). UTSA signed 3 WRs but lost 2 to transfer portal (opportunity). Also found: Dallas Tigers Elite Club (tryouts March 1), USA Track & Field Regional Combine (qualified based on your 40 time)."

---

### 3. Coach Research & Contact Finder
**What it does:**
- Identifies key coaches (position coaches, recruiting coordinators, head coaches)
- Finds verified email addresses
- Researches coaching history, recruiting preferences, recent signees
- Builds contact database with notes

**Data Sources:**
- College athletic staff directories
- LinkedIn, Twitter
- GMTM coach profiles
- Public recruiting databases

**Output:**
- "Found 12 coaches recruiting your position. Top 3: Coach Mike Johnson (Houston WR coach, mike.johnson@uhcougars.com, signed 2 WRs from Texas last year, prefers speed over size). Here are draft emails for each."

## AGENT SDK ARCHITECTURE

**Tools the Agent Needs:**
1. `search_gmtm_athletes` - Find similar athletes
2. `search_gmtm_coaches` - Find coaches by school/position
3. `search_gmtm_events` - Find camps/combines
4. `get_athlete_metrics` - Pull SPARQ data
5. `research_web` - General web search for camps, coaches, teams
6. `send_email` - Send outreach emails
7. `scrape_roster` - Get college roster data
8. `analyze_film` - Video analysis
9. `generate_meal_plan` - Nutrition planning
10. `track_progress` - Log metrics over time

**Resources:**
- GMTM database (primary)
- MaxPreps data
- College athletic websites
- Social media APIs
- Olympic organization databases

**Autonomy Levels:**
1. **Research & Report** - Agent finds info, athlete reviews
2. **Draft & Approve** - Agent writes content, athlete approves before sending
3. **Monitored Autonomy** - Agent acts, athlete receives summaries
4. **Full Autonomy** - Agent handles routine tasks, alerts on critical decisions
